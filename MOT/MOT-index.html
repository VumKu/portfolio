The Arc collection is a response to the modern work situation, which the designer wanted it to provide the shelter and remind people of home. The designer gave this collection a distinct character and made it stand out from typical contract furniture.

The soft silhouette of the sofa strengthens the instantly recognizable character. A thick seam sweeps along the edge of the sofa, like a barrier between the strict and straight outside shape, and the softer sculpted inside. With embracing curves and intricate upholstery details.
